### ðŸš€ Step 1: Phone Preparation (Crucial!)
Before running the script, prepare your phone:

1.  **Enable Developer Mode**:
    - Go to Settings -> About Phone.
    - Tap **Build Number** 7 times rapidly until "You are now a developer!" appears.

2.  **Enable USB Debugging**:
    - Go to Settings -> System -> Developer Options.
    - Turn on **USB Debugging**.
    - **IMPORTANT (Xiaomi Users)**: You must also enable **"USB Install"** and **"USB Debugging (Security Settings)"**. This requires a SIM card and Mi Account login.

3.  **Connect to PC**:
    - Plug in your phone.
    - Check "Always allow from this computer" on the phone's popup and tap OK/Allow.

### ðŸš€ Step 2: One-Click Install
1.  Unzip this package.
2.  Double-click **`Install_Windows.bat`**.
3.  Wait for the "Success" message.

### ðŸš€ Step 3: Final Setup (Manual Fallback)
If the script fails to set permissions:

1.  **Enable Accessibility**:
    - Settings -> Accessibility -> Downloaded Apps.
    - Turn ON **AutoGLM / SIGI**.

2.  **API Key**:
    - Open SIGI App, pull down settings.
    - Enter your Zhipu API Key.
